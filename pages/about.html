🎓 Who I Am

Building at the intersection of biology & technology

I'm Ganesh Mahata, an undergraduate student pursuing B.Tech in Biotechnology at the prestigious Indian Institute of Technology Delhi (IIT Delhi). My journey combines a deep passion for life sciences with a love for engineering, programming, and building smart systems.

Prior to IIT Delhi, I was a student at Jawahar Navodaya Vidyalaya (JNV), Bankura — a centrally funded school that nurtures talent from rural India. That foundation in discipline and excellence shaped my approach to problem-solving.

My goal is to become an engineer who combines biotechnology, embedded systems, artificial intelligence, and software development to create innovative technologies that positively impact society.
